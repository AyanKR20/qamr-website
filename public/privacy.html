Qamr ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Qamr mobile application and related services. By using Qamr, you agree to the practices described here.

Section 01

Information we collect

To make Qamr work — and to keep it real and human — we collect the following:

  • Information you provide — username, email, and profile details.
  • Content — posts, comments, messages, stories, and media you create.
  • Communications — messages between users, and with our support team.
  • Device info — device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage & logs — interactions, diagnostics, and crash reports.
  • Location — approximate location, only if you enable it.
  • Media — images and videos you upload to use app features.
Section 02

How we use your information

  • Operate core features of the app.
  • Enable feeds, messaging, and interactions with other users.
  • Deliver Qamr Pulse and community features.
  • Improve performance and reliability.
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and policy violations.
  • Send notifications you've opted into.
We do not sell your personal data. Ever. That's the whole point.
Section 03

Data sharing

We share only what's necessary to operate Qamr:

  • With other users, based on your own activity and settings.
  • With service providers who handle hosting, notifications, and infrastructure.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect users and the integrity of the platform.

We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

Section 04

Data storage & security

  • Encrypted connections using HTTPS.
  • Secure backend systems with monitored access.
  • Authentication and access controls on all sensitive endpoints.

No system is completely secure, but we work hard to minimize risk and respond quickly to any incident.

Section 05

Data retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

You can request account deletion at any time — we'll walk you through the process.

Section 06

Your rights & controls

  • Access and update your account information.
  • Delete your account permanently.
  • Control notifications via your device settings.
  • Manage and remove your own content.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at qamrapp@gmail.com.

Section 07

Push notifications

We may send you notifications for messages, activity, and important updates. You can disable them at any time in your device settings.

Section 08

Children's privacy

Qamr is not intended for users under 13 (or the applicable age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Section 09

International users

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. By using Qamr, you consent to this processing and to the standards described in this policy.

Section 10

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated effective date at the top of this page.
